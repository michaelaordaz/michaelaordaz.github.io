eLa Certifications

Education Leadership Analytics offers talent assessments for pre-employment or current employees. We can help candidates certify themselves to show that they have the right knowledge and experience to do the job. Meet with our professionals to establish what you expect so that we can create custom questions to help narrow down which candidates are best suited for the job.
